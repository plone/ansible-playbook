Use lsof to make sure we are listening on all expected ports
in all expected ways.

ip4 listeners

    >>> rez = ssh_run('sudo lsof -i4 -i6 -sTCP:LISTEN -Pn | tail -n +2')
    >>> listeners = sorted(set(cut(rez, [0, 2, 8])))
